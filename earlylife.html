Her Story

Early Life & Musical Journey

From Amritsar to National Spotlight

Timeline

A Journey Through Music

Age 7

🎵 The Beginning

Born with a voice that shimmered with promise, Richa Aneja began her musical journey at the tender age of seven in Amritsar, where melodies flowed as naturally as breath. Her voice, both powerful and emotive, drew admiration from teachers, peers, and family members alike.

🎼 Classical Training

Growing up in Amritsar, her home was filled with music and encouragement — especially from her father, Rakesh Kumar, who recognized her gift early. Guided by a guru from the prestigious Gwalior Gharana, Richa immersed herself in classical music, mastering ragas and techniques that would form the bedrock of her artistry.

Age 12–16
Age 16

🎤 Indian Idol Season 3

By sixteen, she was already a seasoned performer, having graced countless school stages and youth festivals. That same year, she auditioned for Indian Idol 3 when the show came to Amritsar — and stunned the judges with her soulful voice. She advanced through every round, earning a coveted spot among the Top 10 finalists nationwide.

📚 Academic Excellence

Even as her fame grew, Richa remained grounded in education. She earned top honors in both her Bachelor's in Music (Vocal) and Master's in Performing Arts, proving that talent and academics could thrive together. Her dedication to both art and scholarship made her a role model for aspiring musicians.

Age 16–22
Age 23+

👩‍🏫 Return as Educator

Returning to her alma mater as an Assistant Professor of Music, Richa brings her performance expertise and academic knowledge to nurture the next generation. Her early life was a blend of grit, grace, and gratitude — laying the foundation for a career that continues to inspire all who hear her sing.

Inspirations

Key Influences

🎭 Gwalior Gharana

A prestigious classical music tradition that emphasizes clarity, precision, and emotional depth. This foundation shaped Richa's approach to music and her lifelong discipline as a performer and teacher.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family Support

Her father Rakesh Kumar's unwavering support and encouragement provided the emotional foundation for her musical pursuits, making family the cornerstone of her journey from the very beginning.

📻 Lata Mangeshkar Legacy

Inspired by legends like Lata Mangeshkar, Richa aspires to carry forward the legacy of soulful Indian singing while adding her own unique voice and emotional authenticity to the tradition.

🌟 National Platform

Indian Idol Season 3 exposed her to millions across India, proving that her talent could resonate on a national scale and opening doors to unprecedented opportunities in music and education.
