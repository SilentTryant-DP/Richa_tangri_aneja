About the Artist

Meet Richa Aneja

In a world filled with fleeting trends and digital noise, Richa Aneja stands as a timeless melody — rooted in tradition, yet always evolving.

An award-winning vocalist, an Indian Idol Top 10 finalist, and an Assistant Professor of Music (Vocal), Richa has spent her life weaving emotion, discipline, and artistry into every note she sings and teaches.

Trained in the prestigious Gwalior Gharana tradition from the age of seven, she has performed on national stages, won regional and national accolades, and continues to inspire students as she pursues her PhD in Music.

✨ At a Glance

  • Music Educator & Performer
  • Finalist — Indian Idol Season 3
  • Former Assistant Professor of Vocal Music
  • Currently pursuing PhD in Music
  • Winner — Harivallabh Sangeet Sammelan
  • Scholarship Holder (Semi-Classical) — Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India
  • Women of the Year — Dainik Bhaskar Award
  • Indian Council of Art & Literature Award
  • Inter-University Winner in Ghazal & Bhajan Gayaki
