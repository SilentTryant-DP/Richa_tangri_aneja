A Celebration of Excellence in Music

Accolades

Her Achievements

🌟

Indian Idol Season 3 — Top 10 Finalist

National TV · 2006

Selected among the Top 10 contestants nationwide on India's most prestigious vocal competition, captivating audiences and judges alike with her soulful classical voice.

🏆

Harivallabh Sangeet Sammelan — Winner

Classical Music · Punjab

Won at the prestigious Harivallabh Sangeet Sammelan, one of the most respected classical music gatherings in North India, a testament to her exceptional mastery of the art.

👩

Women of the Year

Dainik Bhaskar Award

Honoured with the prestigious Women of the Year award by Dainik Bhaskar, one of India's largest circulated newspapers, in recognition of her outstanding contribution to Indian music.

🎓

Ministry of Culture Scholarship

Govt. of India · Semi-Classical Music

Awarded a coveted scholarship in Semi-Classical Music by the Ministry of Culture, Government of India — a recognition of excellence given to the country's most promising musicians.

🎖️

Indian Council of Art & Literature Award

National Recognition

Received the prestigious Indian Council of Art and Literature Award, recognising her significant and lasting contribution to preserving and promoting classical and devotional music traditions.

🏅

Inter-University Winner — Ghazal & Bhajan

University Competition

Won the Inter-University competition in both Ghazal and Bhajan Gayaki categories, showcasing her extraordinary range across the semi-classical and devotional music traditions.
